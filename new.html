Powerful Engine: Equipped with a 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 engine, producing 627 hp and 750 Nm of torque, making it the most powerful production BMW M5 to date.
Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in just 3.0 seconds, showcasing supercar-level performance.
Top Speed: Electronically limited to 305 km/h (190 mph), offering outstanding high-speed capability.

CS-Specific Chassis Tuning: Enhanced suspension, damper tuning, and firmer engine mounts for sharper handling and stability.
Weight Reduction: Extensive use of carbon-fiber-reinforced plastic (CFRP) for the hood, roof, front splitter, rear spoiler, and mirror caps, contributing to a weight reduction of 70 kg compared to the standard M5 Competition.
Performance Tires: Comes standard with Pirelli P Zero Corsa high-performance tires, enhancing grip and cornering precision.

Sport Seats: Features lightweight M Carbon bucket seats with CS badging, providing outstanding support during aggressive driving.
Material Quality: Extensive use of Alcantara, leather, and carbon fiber throughout the cabin, delivering a premium yet sporty feel.
Four-Seater Layout: The rear seats are bucket-style as well, enhancing exclusivity and sporty design.
